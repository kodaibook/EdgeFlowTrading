The EdgeFlow Solution

EdgeFlow merges three pillars into one feedback loop with KPIs:

  • MentorAI — adaptive curriculum, step-by-step explanations, targeted corrections, knowledge checks.
  • Demo Arena — live market simulations, AI evaluation pre/post execution, risk rule enforcement, auto journal.
  • $EFLOW Utility — rewards for verified progress, staking, premium modules, governance, deflationary burn.

MentorAI Loop

  • Modular curriculum tied to market context; quizzes and tasks validate understanding.
  • Personalized study plan based on performance and error patterns.

KPI: lesson score, quiz accuracy, weekly consistency, accepted corrections, behavior score.

Demo Arena

  • Real-time data; simulated orders with strict risk rules and plan validation.
  • AI checks plan quality and adherence; meta-tagged auto journal.

KPI: RRR, expectancy, win rate, max drawdown, plan adherence, decision latency, discipline breaks.

$EFLOW Economy

  • Rewards for validated learning and correct execution.
  • Premium features, staking, and community governance.
  • Deflationary burn 0.5–1% per transaction.

Off-chain → On-chain bridge: monthly conversion of in-app points to $EFLOW under dynamic limits and anti-abuse checks.
